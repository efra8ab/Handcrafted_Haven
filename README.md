## Team members
- Efrain Ochoa


## 📌 Overview
**Handcrafted Haven** is an innovative web application designed to connect artisans and crafters with customers who value unique, handmade products.

It serves as a **virtual marketplace** where creators can showcase their work, tell their stories, and sell their handcrafted items. The platform promotes:
- 🌱 Sustainable consumption  
- 🤝 Community building  
- 🎨 Appreciation for craftsmanship  

The goal is to make discovering and purchasing handmade goods easier, while empowering creators with a broader audience.

---

## ⚙️ Features

### 🧑‍🎨 Seller Profiles
- Authenticated sellers can create personalized profiles  
- Showcase their craftsmanship and story  
- Display a curated collection of their products  

### 📦 Product Listings
- Add handcrafted items with:
  - Descriptions  
  - Pricing  
  - Images  
- Users can browse and filter products by:
  - Category  
  - Price range  
  - Other relevant criteria  

### ⭐ Reviews & Ratings
- Users can:
  - Leave ratings  
  - Write product reviews
